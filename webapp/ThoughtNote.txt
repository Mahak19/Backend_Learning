This project is created to show the sales app functionality. This app consists of various pages, each one of them has it's own use.

Navbar Component: The Navbar component provides navigation links to different pages of the application, including adding sales, viewing top sales, checking today's total revenue, logging in, and registering.

Login Page: The Login page allows users to authenticate themselves by providing their email and password. Upon successful login, users gain access to the application's features.

Registration Page: The Registration page enables new users to sign up for the application by providing their first name, last name, email, and password. Once registered, users can log in to access the application.

Add Sales Page: The Add Sales page allows users to input details about a new sale, including the product name, quantity, and sale amount. This feature facilitates the addition of new sales records to the system.

Top Sales Page: The Top Sales page displays a list of the top five sales records based on their revenue. Users can quickly view which products generated the highest sales amounts.

Total Revenue Page: The Total Revenue page presents the total revenue generated by all sales recorded for the current day. This information provides users with insights into the overall performance of sales activities for the day.

Some Key Features are as follows: 

User Authentication: Users can securely log in and register for the application, ensuring data privacy and security.
Sales Management: The application enables users to add new sales records, view top sales, and monitor total revenue, supporting effective sales management.
Responsive Design: The user interface is designed to be responsive, ensuring optimal viewing and interaction experiences across various devices and screen sizes.
